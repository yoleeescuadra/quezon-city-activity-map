How to read the map

In simple terms

Red areas lean more toward daytime activity, blue areas lean more toward nighttime activity, and gray areas are closer in relative strength.

What you can explore

  • Switch between overall orientation, daytime strength, and nighttime strength.
  • Click a cell to compare qualitative day and night bands.
  • Open the three reference areas to see the plausibility story.

Important limits

These are relative signals within Quezon City. They are not population, mobility, customer-volume, or foot-traffic estimates.

Privacy-safe view

The map excludes source analysis, formulas, weights, thresholds, raw feature counts, exact scores, and cell-level analytical inputs.
